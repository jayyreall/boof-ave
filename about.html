Meet the Collective

Boof Ave™ is a cross-disciplinary studio rooted in Oakland, operating globally to bring editorial-grade storytelling to brands that want to move culture forward. We believe the best ideas feel handcrafted, collaborative, and a little rebellious.

Our Story

The Ave Origin

Founded by creative director Jae "Boof" Benton, the studio started as a pop-up warehouse photo lab and grew into a full-spectrum creative partner for founders, fashion houses, and community organizations.

Strategy First

Every engagement begins with a deep dive workshop where we map your audience, tone, and outcomes. That intent fuels sharper creative.

Integrated Craft

Our crew spans creative strategy, design, photo, video, experiential, and post, so your narrative stays cohesive across every medium.

Culture Forward

We collaborate with stylists, musicians, and technologists to ensure each concept feels alive in the communities you serve.
