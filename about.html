Meet the Founders

Boof Ave™ is a cross-disciplinary studio with roots in Atlanta, Georgia, Baltimore, Maryland, and Chicago, Illinois. Our collective consists of musicians, artists, photographers, and more operating nationally to bring editorial-grade storytelling to brands that desire to move culture forward. We believe that the best ideas require equitable collaboration and a personal, handcrafted touch.

Jarel Robinson

CEO & Founder — Rapper, producer, master photographer and videographer behind Reall Archives™, co-founder of clothing brand No Pride™, and pioneer at Boof Ave™.

Akosua Johnson

COO & Creative Director — Resident visionary at Boof Ave™ and multimedia visual artist whose mediums include pencil, acrylic, watercolor, clay, digital art, and poetry.
